Learn More About Fikua

Fikua is a non-profit organization dedicated to facilitating and providing technology guidance related to self-sovereign identity and digital identity for any organization aiming to create and implement a digital identity system.

Our mission is to empower individuals and communities by offering resources, tools, and education to foster innovation and sustainable development.

We collaborate with diverse partners to promote ethical technology use and create positive social impacts through our projects.
